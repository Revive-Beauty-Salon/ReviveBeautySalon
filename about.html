About REVIVE Beauty Salon

Our Story

Founded with a passion for beauty and wellness, REVIVE Beauty Salon has been serving the Navi Mumbai community with exceptional beauty services since our inception. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where every client feels pampered, rejuvenated, and truly beautiful.

Located in the heart of Airoli, we have built our reputation on providing personalized care, using premium products, and employing only the most skilled and certified beauty professionals. Every treatment at REVIVE is designed to not just enhance your outer beauty, but to revive your inner confidence and well-being.
