How to Reach Us

Easy directions to find our salon

By Car

Take the Eastern Express Highway and exit at Airoli. Our salon is easily accessible from the main road with parking available nearby.

By Train

Nearest railway station is Rabale on the Harbour Line. Take an auto-rickshaw or taxi for a 10-minute ride to our salon.

By Bus

Several NMMT buses connect to Airoli Sector 20D. Get down at the nearest bus stop and walk 3 minutes to our salon.
